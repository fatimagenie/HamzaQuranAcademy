## Brand & Style

The design system is built upon the concept of "Illuminated Knowledge." It balances the weight of tradition with the clarity of modern education. The visual narrative centers on serenity, growth, and divine excellence, targeting students, parents, and scholars seeking a professional Quranic education.

The design style is **Corporate / Modern** with **Minimalist** influences, subtly enhanced by **High-Contrast** gold accents. It avoids over-decoration in favor of purposeful whitespace, allowing the sacred nature of the content to breathe. The atmosphere is scholarly and precise, yet warm and welcoming through the use of organic textures and soft light.

## Colors

The palette is rooted in a deep "Deep Emerald" primary color, representing the traditional connection to growth and spiritual vitality. This is contrasted with "Antique Gold," used sparingly for accents, icons, and primary call-to-actions to denote quality and excellence. 

The background relies on "Scholar’s Parchment" (a refined cream) instead of pure white to reduce eye strain during long reading sessions and to evoke a sense of heritage. Text is set in a "Midnight Slate" to ensure high legibility while maintaining a softer contrast than pure black.

## Typography

This design system utilizes a sophisticated typographic pairing. **Source Serif 4** is the primary choice for headings and display text, providing an authoritative, literary feel that honors the tradition of Quranic scholarship. 

**Work Sans** serves as the functional workhorse for body text, navigation, and labels. Its professional and grounded nature ensures that complex educational information remains accessible and clear for students of all ages. Large display titles on mobile use a reduced scale to maintain a premium editorial feel without causing excessive line breaks.

## Layout & Spacing

The layout follows a **Fixed Grid** philosophy on desktop to maintain a contained, focused reading experience similar to a physical book. A 12-column grid is used with generous gutters to create a sense of calm. 

Vertical rhythm is governed by a strict 8px baseline, with larger "stack" increments used to group related educational modules. On mobile, margins are tightened to 20px, and the grid collapses to a single column, prioritizing a linear, distraction-free flow for mobile learning and administration.

## Elevation & Depth

To maintain a serene and trustworthy aesthetic, this design system avoids heavy shadows. Depth is achieved primarily through **Tonal Layers**. 

Interactive surfaces use very subtle, low-opacity **Ambient Shadows** (Primary color tinted at 5% opacity) to suggest elevation without breaking the "flat" scholarly feel. Backgrounds are primarily the "Scholar's Parchment" color, while interactive cards and modals use pure white to pop forward. Thin, 1px borders in a lighter shade of the primary green are used to define boundaries in complex data views.

## Shapes

The shape language is **Soft** and intentional. A 4px (0.25rem) radius is the standard for most UI elements, providing enough softness to feel welcoming without losing the professional, structured appearance required for an academic institution. 

Larger containers like cards use an 8px radius, while circular treatments are reserved strictly for student avatars and specific progress indicators to maintain a clear visual metaphor for personal growth and completion.

## Components

### Buttons
Primary buttons are solid Emerald Green with white text, utilizing the Soft corner radius. Secondary buttons use an Antique Gold outline with Gold text for excellence-focused actions like "Join Class" or "View Certificate."

### Cards
Educational cards use a white background with a 1px "Emerald-10" border. They feature a subtle gold top-border (2px) for featured courses or announcements to provide visual distinction.

### Inputs & Fields
Form fields utilize a subtle cream background with a bottom-only border that thickens and changes to Emerald when focused. This mimics the feeling of writing on a lined manuscript.

### Progress Indicators
Progress bars for Quranic memorization use a two-tone green approach: a light mint background with a deep emerald fill, often accompanied by a small gold star icon upon completion of a Surah.

### Lists
Student and lesson lists are separated by thin, light-grey horizontal rules with generous vertical padding (stack-sm) to ensure the interface remains uncluttered and easy to navigate for older users.
